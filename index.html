At Oma's Restaurant, we bring you the true taste of Arabian hospitality. With over 6 years of culinary excellence, we've become a beloved dining destination across Kerala. Our journey has been driven by passion, flavor, and community.

Our Facilities

Built for Your Special Moments

Every detail is designed to make your visit comfortable, memorable, and stress-free.

Stress-Free Parking

The widest parking area in the Panoor region. Drive in effortlessly, even during peak hours. No more circling around — your spot is always ready.

Children's Play Area

Let the kids play safely while you enjoy your meal in peace. Our dedicated, supervised play zone keeps little ones entertained and happy.

Party & Banquet Facilities

Host your family functions, birthdays, and corporate events in our dedicated spaces. Custom menus & décor available upon request.
